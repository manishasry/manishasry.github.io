I'm Manisha

I’m a UX Researcher and UX Designer with 2+ years of experience in Digital Marketing, having worked with clients from business verticals like healthcare, education, F&B, elearning and ecommerce. I was born in India. I’m an IAF brat, educated at Kendriya Vidyalayas located at various locations in the country. I earned a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Engineering from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere. I am currently pursuing Masters of Design from PDPM Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing, Jabalpur. I enjoy working in multi-disciplinary and fast-moving teams. I have helped businesses by strengthening their online presence and simplifying customer experience. I strongly advocate for customer centric solutions and businesses and love contributing to growth driven teams.
