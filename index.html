Hello! I am Manisha.       

I am a Master of Design final year student at PDPM IIITDM Jabalpur studying multidisciplinary design with focus on visual ergonomics and interaction design. At present, I am working towards earning my Google UX Design Professional Certificate.

In my free time, I am either scribbling, reading, writing or planning my next travel.
